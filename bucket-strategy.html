Bucket Strategy

Retirement corpus framework with bucket-level protection and growth

Corpus Plan At Retirement

Initial SWP target is Rs 1,00,000 per month (Rs 12,00,000 per year).

B1 is internally split as B1A (Emergency Untouchable) and B1B (Medical Reserve).

Bucket construction is based on annual-expense multiples:

Bucket Multiple Target Corpus Role
B1 4x Rs 48L Insurance bucket (B1A emergency + B1B medical)
B2 3x Rs 36L Income runway
B3 5x Rs 60L Crash buffer
B4 40x Rs 4.8Cr Long-term growth engine
Total 52x Rs 6.24Cr Full retirement corpus

What This System Achieves

  1. SWR from B4 is 2.5% (Rs 12L on Rs 4.8Cr).
  2. Sequence-of-returns risk is reduced with B2 + B3 = 8 years of expense buffer.
  3. Monthly expenses are separated from emergency capital by paying regular SWP from B2.
  4. B1 remains protected by using B1A only for emergencies and B1B for planned medical needs.

Withdrawal Flow Policy

  1. Regular monthly SWP is paid from B2 and indexed at 6% each year.
  2. B1A is emergency-only and remains untouchable in normal years.
  3. B1B handles planned medical expenses with annual review and refill if needed.
  4. B3 is activated in prolonged market stress when B2 refill from B4 is paused.
  5. B4 is used for planned yearly refill, not panic withdrawals.

Retirement Execution Rules

  1. Start SWP at Rs 1L/month and increase by 6% every year.
  2. In bear markets, pause B4 withdrawals and run expenses through B2 plus B3 buffer.
  3. In normal and strong markets, refill B2 annually from B4 using predefined rebalance windows.
  4. From year 5 onward, allow around Rs 4L withdrawal from B3 for travel and hobbies only when guardrails permit.
  5. From year 5 onward, budget around Rs 2L/year medical withdrawal from B1B and grow this budget at medical inflation.
  6. If B4 drawdown exceeds 20%, freeze discretionary withdrawals until recovery.

Built with patience, discipline, and compounding.
